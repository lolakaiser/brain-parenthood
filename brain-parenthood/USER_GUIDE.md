## Module System

Brain Parenthood consists of 12 weekly modules, each focusing on different aspects of team resilience.

### Module Overview

1. **Week 1 - Kick Off**: Establish baseline, set goals, and learn Brain Parenthood concepts
2. **Week 2-3 - Mindfulness**: Focus on mindfulness practices (2x daily)
3. **Week 4-5 - Resilience**: Resilience workshop and exercises (3x weekly)
4. **Week 6-7 - Communication**: Active communication training (1x daily)
5. **Week 8-9 - Innovation**: Apply skills to real challenges (3x daily)
6. **Week 10-11 - Measurement**: Progress assessment and coaching (2x weekly)
7. **Week 12 - Wrap-up**: Final reflection and celebration

### Accessing Modules

- From the **Home Page**: Click on any available module card
- From the **Dashboard**: Use the "Next Steps" section
- Only available modules can be accessed (currently Module 1)

## Module 1: Foundation

Module 1 is your introduction to Brain Parenthood and consists of 4 steps:

### Step 1: Overview
- Learn what Brain Parenthood is
- Understand why team resilience matters
- Review the program objectives

### Step 2: Baseline Assessment
A series of questions to establish your starting point:
1. **Team Stress Level**: Overall stress across your team (1-10 scale)
2. **Individual Stress Level**: Your personal stress level (1-10 scale)
3. **Team Productivity**: Current productivity rating (1-10 scale)
4. **Communication Quality**: How well your team communicates (1-10 scale)
5. **Work-Life Balance**: Current balance rating (1-10 scale)
6. **Team Size**: Number of people on your team
7. **Primary Challenges**: Open text describing current challenges

**Tips for Assessment:**
- Be honest - this data helps tailor the program
- Use the full scale (don't just use middle values)
- Think about the past 2-4 weeks when answering
- For text responses, be specific and detailed

### Step 3: Goal Setting
Define what you want to achieve in 12 weeks:
1. **Stress Reduction Goal**: Specific target (e.g., "Reduce team stress from 7 to 4")
2. **Productivity Goal**: Measurable objective (e.g., "Increase productivity by 25%")
3. **Communication Goal**: Clear improvement target (e.g., "Daily check-ins")
4. **Personal Development Goal**: What you personally want to achieve
5. **Team Development Goal**: What your team wants to achieve together
6. **Success Metrics**: How you'll measure success at the end

**Tips for Goal Setting:**
- Use SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound)
- Be realistic but ambitious
- Focus on what matters most to your team
- Think about both quantitative and qualitative outcomes

### Step 4: Complete
Review your submission and see what's next.

### Navigation

- **Back Button**: Return to previous question/step
- **Next Button**: Move to next question (enabled when current question is answered)
- **Progress Bar**: Shows your position in the assessment
- **Question Counter**: Displays current question number out of total

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Problem**: Invalid username or password error

**Solutions**:
- Double-check your username (case-sensitive)
- Verify your password
- Ensure you've created an account
- Try clearing your browser cache

### Module Won't Load

**Problem**: Module page doesn't display correctly

**Solutions**:
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Check your internet connection

### Progress Not Saving

**Problem**: Responses or progress not persisting

**Solutions**:
- Ensure you're logged in
- Check internet connection
- Complete all required fields
- Click "Next" or "Continue" buttons to save

### Layout Issues

**Problem**: Page looks broken or margins are missing

**Solutions**:
- Try a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled
- Clear browser cache
- Update your browser to the latest version

### Button Not Working

**Problem**: Can't click Next or submit

**Possible Causes**:
- Required field not filled in
- Input validation failed
- Network connection issue

**Solutions**:
- Check that all fields are completed
- For sliders, ensure you've moved them
- For text fields, enter valid data
- Refresh the page if needed
